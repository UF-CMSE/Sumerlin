2012 Symposium

The 7th annual symposium will be held October 22-24, 2012 at the Hilton Sonoma Wine Country in Santa Rosa, CA. The Hilton rests on 13 acres of beautifully landscaped grounds with panoramic views of Sonoma Valley and is centrally located to over 330 Sonoma County wineries. Just north of the Bay Area, Santa Rosa is conveniently reached from the San Francisco (SFO), Oakland (OAK), and San Jose (SJC) airports. Alternatively, the Sonoma County Airport (STS), which is serviced by Alaska Airlines, is just 15 minutes away.
