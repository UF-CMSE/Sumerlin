William Brooks

Education:                   
Ph.D. Candidate, Chemistry, University of Florida

B.S., Polymer Science, University of Southern Mississippi, Hattiesburg, MS, 2010
Research Synopsis: Sol-gel Modification of Nafion® and Subsequent Characterization for Use in Electrochemical Actuators
Advisor: Dr. Kenneth Mauritz

Current Research Project and Objectives:
My research revolves around the synthesis and characterization of stimuli responsive and dynamic-covalent polymer systems via reversible addition-fragmentation chain transfer (RAFT) polymerization.  I also investigate the use of microwave-irradiation to quickly synthesize materials and further the understanding of the role microwave-irradiation plays in enhanced polymerization kinetics.

Publications:
